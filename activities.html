Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of private hiking trails, including a trail to a private beach. Guided hiking tours of the redwoods are available every morning.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. Half-day and full-day guided kayak tours are available.

Bird Watching

The North Coast is a bird watcher's paradise. Guests have spotted Great Blue Herons, Snowy Egrets, and even Bald Eagles.
