The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are based on the traditional design used by nomadic peoples of Central Asia. Each yurt is a circular, framed tent covered with durable canvas.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is equipped with a queen-size bed with down comforter, a small sitting area, and a private deck with ocean views.
