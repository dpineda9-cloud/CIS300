The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt? Our luxury yurts are based on the ancient design of the Mongols. Each yurt is a canvas-covered circular structure that is elevated off the ground with a wooden deck.

  • Fully enclosed with canvas walls
  • Sky light and heating
  • Comfortable beds
